A New Breed of Web Apps

Transition seamlessly between desktop, tablet and smartphone with our new Widget technology. Achieve real OS and device independence for true BYOD. Eliminate the need to build multiple native apps for many devices reducing time-to-market and costs. Integrate with any system, cloud service and other Widgets for a unique, powerful network of collaborating functions.

  •  Resize automatically for desktops and mobile devices
  •  Interactive, real-time data share feature using websockets
  •  Standardized full custom action menus
  •  Built-in documentation for self-teaching software
  •  Native mode for full screen engagement on smaller devices
  •  Integration API for systems, services and other Widgets

A Private Marketplace

Administer your entire organization from a centralized, private Volute Marketplace. Employees, partners and vendors find every Widget they need from your Marketplace on any desktop and mobile device from anywhere. The Volute Marketplace is your cloud solution for all your company applications accessed securely and simply.

  •  Assign access roles and Widgets on the fly
  •  Categorize Widgets however you choose
  •  Create your own Widget bundles
  •  Publish Widgets to a private or public marketplace
  •  Access your marketplace from any desktop or mobile device
  •  Rapid prototyping for fast application assembly

Fluid Dashboards

Choose Widgets from your private Volute marketplace and arrange them on your own customizable dashboards. Engage with Widgets specific to your tasks, share real-time interactive data and create unique dashboards for each of your devices. Volute dashboards are your spaces to add, remove and arrange Widgets to enhance your personal productivity style.

  •  Control all aspects of your dashboard creation
  •  Transition seamlessly between desktop & mobile device
  •  Choose your own themes
  •  Arrange Widgets in any position
  •  Hide and show Widgets on the fly
  •  Navigate quickly to any dashboard

Anatomy of a Widget

  • Drag Handle: grab and arrange however you choose
  • Action Menu: full custom actions for every requirement
  • Share: send an entire live Widget in real-time to peers
  • Built-in Help: flip your Widget to reveal documentation
  • Offline: offline mode to stay productive with no connection
  • Launch: drill deeper into your Widget for new insights
  • Rotate Faces: turn to reveal new Widget user interfaces
